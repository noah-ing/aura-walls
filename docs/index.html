Privacy Policy

Aura Walls respects your privacy.

  • Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.
  • Favorites: Your favorited wallpapers are stored locally on your device using SwiftData. This data never leaves your device.
  • AI Generation: When you use the AI wallpaper generator, your text prompt is sent to Hugging Face's API to generate the image. No prompts are stored or logged by us.
  • Ads: Free users see ads served by Google AdMob. Google may collect device identifiers for ad personalization. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Analytics: We do not use any analytics or tracking tools.
  • Subscriptions: Subscription purchases are handled entirely by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information.
